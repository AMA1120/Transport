Welcome to Badminton Club

Badminton Club provides an excellent opportunity to play badminton and socialise in a friendly and relaxed atmosphere. Players have the oppurtunity to play either for leisure or more competitively - with five sessions a week, there is plenty of opportunity to improve general fitness and enhance your game. No experience or equipment is required to join us, all shuttles are provided and rackets are available to members during sessions. The club caters for new beginners through to those who play at on the international stage. Beginners have the oppurtunity to receive informal coaching from our performance athletes in a relaxed and friendly environment either through our dedicated Boost Badminton program or at any club session just by chatting to the exec. Players looking for more cometitive play can choose to join our Active teams to compete directly against other members of the university
